**Marduk** is one of the four [Technology
Plants](../locations/Technology_Plant.md) on [Ishundar](../locations/Ishundar.md)
and is in the South inside grid H15.

It has [Lattice](../terminology/Lattice.md) Links to:

- [Interlink Facility](../terminology/Interlink.md)
  [Neti](Neti.md)
- [Amp Station](../locations/Amp_Station.md) [Enkidu](Enkidu.md)
- A [Broadcast](../terminology/Broadcast.md) [Warpgate](../locations/Warpgate.md)
- A [Geowarp](../locations/Geowarp.md)

It is a [Hack-and-Hold](../terminology/Hack-and-Hold.md)
[Facility](../locations/Facilities.md) with an [Air
tower](../locations/Air_tower.md) to its north, inside its
[SOI](../locations/Sphere_of_Influence.md).

Elevation: 70.5m

## Trivia

_Marduk_ (mar'dook) was the name of a Mesopotamian god and the patron
god of Babylon. See the [Wikipedia](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marduk)
article.
