The [Cavern](Cavern.md "wikilink") Ancient Technology Plant can be found in
each of the 6 [Core Combat](Core_Combat.md "wikilink") Caverns. There are 2
AT Plants in each cavern and are always paired with a nearby [Redoubt
Building](Redoubt_Building.md "wikilink")

The AT Plant features:

- An [Ancient Control Console](Ancient_Control_Console.md "wikilink")
- An [Ancient Ground Vehicle
  Terminal](Ancient_Ground_Vehicle_Terminal.md "wikilink")
- 2 [Ancient Air Vehicle
  Terminals](Ancient_Air_Vehicle_Terminal.md "wikilink")
- [Teleporters](Teleporter.md "wikilink")
