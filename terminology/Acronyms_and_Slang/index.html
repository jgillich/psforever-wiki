    Acronyms and Slang

    PlanetSide, like all games, seems to have it's own language when referring to items or places in game. This is a listing of known acronyms. If there is a term you know that is not listed, feel free to add it into the proper category.

    For general gaming slang or leetspeak (not PlanetSide-specific), see Leet.

    4th Empire

    Derogatory term for (real or perceived) players who join the Empire with the current advantage.

    A

    AA

    Anti Aircraft (MAXes, BFRs, Wall Turrets, Cerberus Turrets, and Skyguards.)

    Aircav

    Air Cavalry

    ammo

    Short for ammunition.

    AMS

    Advanced Mobile Station. Name for the mobile spawn point vehicle.

    ANT

    Advanced Nanite Transport. The vehicle that carries NTUs to distribute to NTU Silos.

    ANT drop

    The action of ejecting an Advanced Nanite Transport from a Galaxy or Lodestar near an NTU Silo. Also another name for ANT run.

    ANT run

    A mission to refill depleted or critical NTU Silos, often utilizing a Galaxy or Lodestar to speed up the travel to and from Warpgates.

    Assfire

    Refers to the Starfire MAX

    AT

    Ancient-Tech. Having to do with Core Combat assets.

    AV

    Anti-Vehicular certification, MAXes, or weapons.

    B

    BD

    short for Back Door, referring to the back door of a base.

    Badge

    Refers to Merit Commendations, because one can wear shoulder patches denoting one's merits.

    Bailure

    The act of fighting in a Mosquito and then dropping onto the target after your vehicle is destroyed. Then repeating this single tactic until you log. or One who bails from a plane before they would die, denying the attacker, usually anti-air, the kill.

    Bait

    Grouping of enemy (or friendly) troops that are being susceptible to a particular attack. Flavors include OS Bait, Liberator Bait, and Thumper Bait.

    Bang Buggy

    Slang term for the Terran Republic Marauder

    Bang Bus

    Common term used when referring to a Sunderer variant troop transport. Also called Sundy, Funbus, Mack Truck, and Winnebego.

    BEP

    Abbreviation for Battle Experience Points

    BFR Hunter (Biffer Hunter)

    A solo Infantry whose sole intent is to hunt enemy BFRs and destroy them, either by dropping on them from above, or rushing them from a base. Sometimes work in pairs, but rarely in groups of 3 or more.

    Biffer

    BFR (BattleFrame Robotics)

    Big Bird

    A slang term for a New Conglomerate player at BR14 to BR24. Refers to the predominately yellow armor suit.

    Bluebie

    Slang term for a (often new) New Conglomerate player, BR1 to BR6. It is a reference to the predominately blue armor suit.

    Boomer

    Remotely detonated charge created from an ACE and planted by Combat Engineers.

    BR

    Battle Rank. See CR.

    Brick

    Slang for the Galaxy dropship.

    BEP

    Battle Experience Points The experience points you collect from killing enemies, capturing bases and exploring the world of Planetside.

    C

    Caver, Ferret

    Soldiers who battle in the Caverns.

    CC

    Control Console, the capture point for bases and towers. Also refers to Core Combat.

    Click-Click

    A historical way radio operators would acknowledge receipt of a transmission by sending 2 short "clicks" of static ("Everyone understand?" reply: "cc").

    CEP

    Command Experience Points The experience points you collect from being a squad leader.

    CE

    Combat Engineering, common referrence in the field for Mines, Spitfire turrrets. ("Get some CE down at this base please!")

    Cloaker

    Player who uses the Infiltration Suit

    CoF

    System used to determine where shots are fired. See Cone of fire

    Certifications

    CN

    Chinese player

    cont lock, contlock

    Continental lock or Continent lock.

    CR/BR

    Command Rank and Battle Rank.

    Crackhammer

    A common TR term for the NC Jackhammer.

    Crash

    An assault from a large group of the exact same unit all in one large wave. Most common variety being a MAX Crash

    CTD

    Crash to Desktop ("Sorry, I'm back after a CTD")

    CY

    short for courtyard, referring to the area between the facility's structure and the surrounding base walls.

    CyssorSide

    A derogatory term for fighting on Cyssor. Due to the size of the continent and base links, battles usually last a long time (sometimes multiple days) and are often a three way.

    Chiggers

    Group of defenders that have dug-in and proven difficult to remove.

    D

    DC, DCMAX

    Dual-Cycler, TR Anti Infantry MAX.

    DC'ed

    disconnected due to connection problems.

    deci

    Decimator Anti-vehicle weapon.

    deli

    Deliverer troop transport.

    Deployables

    Name for items created with the ACE, such as Mines, Spitfires, Boomers, and Motion Sensors. Often interchanged with "CE."

    Drain

    The process of damaging an enemy base causing the base to deplete all of it's NTUs for auto-repairing its structures. (JamQ Guide on how to drain a Base.)

    Duck Walk

    A celebratory "dance" players sometimes do after a base capture (or if there are no enemies to kill while waiting for the timer). Usually done by forming a line with other players and crouch walking while doing an emote, usually the cabbagepatch or dunno emotes. Also called a Conga Line.

    Es

    Esamir.

    Empire Specific Anti-Vehicular weapons (Lancer, Phoenix, and Striker).

    ESMA

    Empire Specific Medium Assault weapons (Pulsar, Gauss, and Cycler). Not to be confused with EAMS.

    Eterm

    Equipment Terminal.

    Fagrider

    Another name for the Vanu Sovereignty Magrider.

    Fagrocket

    Another name for the Phoenix rockets.

    Facility Linked Benefits. Refers to base benefits that link through the lattice to other facilities.

    PlanetSide Reserves

    Fodderling

    ForumSide

    Refers to the "game" of posting alot on the PlanetSide Forums.

    Fraggle

    Referring to a player who loves to play in the caverns. ("We need some Fraggles to protect that cavern lock in Byblos!")

    Fragstab

    The act of throwing a Fragmentation grenade quickly followed by a Melee-Boosted knife attack to kill an enemy almost instantly.

    G

    Gal

    Abbreviation for the Galaxy

    Gal Gunship / GG

    Abbreviation for the Galaxy Gunship

    Gal Bomb

    Charging a (typically unloaded) Galaxy into a target

    Gal drop

    A hotdrop of soldiers from a Galaxy class transport. MAXes and Rexos expected.

    Gal Spam

    Multiple Galaxies being used a "Gal Bombs"

    Gaybot

    Another name for BFRs (BattleFrame Robotics), used by people who are against BFR's

    Gen

    Generator

    Gen Drop / "Drop The Gens!"

    Common referrence to the tactic of destroying (Dropping) the Generator ("Drop their generator so we can break their defense!"). In context of defense, an enemy Galaxy dropping troops near the generator, especially at Bio Labs ("Gen drop incoming! Defend the generator!").

    Gen Hold

    Furthering the Gen Drop tactic of destroying the base's Generator (usually a Technology Plant) and holding the Generator Room to prevent the enemy from repairing it.

    Ghostbuster

    A player with the expertise of hunting and killing cloakers.

    Ghost Hack

    Hacking a continent with little to no resistance from the enemy.

    Glue

    Armor Canisters for armor or vehicular repair. Also the act of repairing. ("I'm going to glue the gen.")

    GlueGun

    Referring to the Nano Dispenser

    Granny Hacking

    Hacking without the Hacking or Advanced Hacking abilities.

    Grunt

    See: Infantry

    H

    HA

    Heavy Assault certification or weapon.

    Hax0r , Haxxor , Haxx0r

    An Advanced Hacker or someone that uses illegal hacks.

    HART

    Refers to the HART shuttle, its drop pods, its launch building, or that mode of transportation in general. "I'm going to HART in rather than use the warpgates."

    Hotdrop

    The act of flying over a base, tower, or any other location where the "LZ" (landing zone, dropzone) is otherwise "hot" (under fire) and unavailable for landing and dropping the contents of a Galaxy or Lodestar

    Har/Harry

    Harasser vehicle.

    HUD

    Hunter

    Infy, Infil

    Cloaked enemy, also refers to the Infiltration Suit.

    Infil Suit

    Abbreviation for the Infiltration Suit

    Instagib

    The process of being killing or being killed instantly. Most commonly used when the target was at full health and was killed in a single attack. "That Jackhammer just instagibbed me!"

    J

    Jihad

    The act of attacking a BFR as a solo Infantry. This assault promises the death of the Infantry, but it may also result in the target BFR being destroyed. Also used in reference to attacking a superior target, groups of targets, or an unattackable target (ie tree or wall) using means that guarantee the death of the attacker almost instantly.

    JJ

    "Jump Jets". Term from BattleTech that can be applied to VS MAXes with their JumpJet systems, and the Flight-Variant BFRs with their JumpJets.

    Juice

    "Armor Juice" refers to the Armor Canisters used by the Nano Dispenser, and "Medic Juice" refers to Health Canisters used by the Medical Applicator.

    K

    KAAOS Drop

    Named after the Emerald TR outfit KAAOS. Using Air Cavalry vehicles in large groups to drop on an objective, similar to a Galaxy drop, but twice the air speed of a Galaxy. This drop would then be followed by a surge run to a specific objective in the base.

    Killwhore

    Player whose only purpose of the game is (or appears to be) the rapid increase of their kill count, whether highly skilled at it or not.

    Kitchen (aka Barracks)

    Refers to the lounge that is behind the spawn room

    KSer

    Spam

    The act of firing constantly at a target, often to the point of emptying the clip, regardless of whether or not there are actual enemies in that area ("spamming a back door with Maelstroms", "rocket-spamming infantry")- Also used to describe chat flooding ("quit spamming global chat!")

    TK

    Teamkill - when one intentionally (or unintentionally) kills a teammate. Often happens with OSes. Also Total Kills

    TKer

    Someone who makes a habit of killing teammates, or trying to. They may be attempting to sabotage, or incite other players to kill them and thusly earn grief points.

    TS

    TeamSpeak, (Teamspeak RC2) free 3rd-party voice-over-ip communication software. Can also refer to (although rare) TeamSound, a similar voice communication software product.

    TSO

    TeamSpeak Overlay, a tool that shows a semi-transparent overlay whilst playing a game. The overlay lights up whenever someone talks, showing their name so you know who's talking.

    TOH

    Time on Hack, how much time is remaining for the facility's hack to complete.

    TTK
